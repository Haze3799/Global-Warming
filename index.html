What can we do about it?

At home

Using less energy is really important when is comes to preventing climate change.Turning og lights when not in use, washing laundry with cold as aposed to hot water and hanging clothes to dry instead of using the tumple dryer. These are all extreamly easy and efficiant of saving energy.

transportation

Most vehicles use petrol or diesel. By walking or cycling instead of driving we can reduce the amount of green house gass emissions, and also help your health and fitness.

Swiching to an electric vehicle

With more and cheaper models coming on the market you have the option of purchasing an electric car in stead of tradition cars using petrol or diesel. Doing this can reduce air pulution and green house gass emissions.
